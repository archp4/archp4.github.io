About Me

Welcome to my portfolio website! I am a dedicated and passionate individual with a solid foundation in Information Technology. My journey in IT began with a Diploma from Bhailalbhai and Bhikhabhai Institute of Technology, where I gained valuable insights into the fundamentals of technology and its applications. Building upon this foundation, I pursued a Bachelor's degree at Devang Patel Institute of Advanced Technology and Research (CHARUSAT University), where I delved deeper into various aspects of IT, honing my skills and expanding my knowledge base.

During my academic journey, I embarked on a hands-on learning experience as an App Developer intern at Mitra Fintech, a dynamic startup. Over the course of 4-5 months, I collaborated with a talented team, contributing to the development of innovative mobile applications. This internship not only provided me with practical experience but also enhanced my problem-solving abilities and teamwork skills.

Currently, I am furthering my education at Humber College, pursuing a post-secondary certificate program in Information Technology Solutions. This program is equipping me with advanced technical expertise and industry-relevant skills, preparing me to thrive in the ever-evolving field of technology.

In addition to my academic pursuits, I am actively seeking part-time opportunities to apply my knowledge and skills in real-world settings. I am eager to explore new challenges and contribute to innovative projects while continuing my academic journey. With a strong educational background, practical experience, and a passion for technology, I am committed to making a meaningful impact in the IT industry.
